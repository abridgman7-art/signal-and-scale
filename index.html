Questions, answered

Everything you wanted to know.

Still curious? Book a 30-minute call and we'll answer anything specific to your business.

How is Signal & Scale different from a regular agency?

We run AI at every layer — creative, targeting, content, CRO — and pair it with senior strategists. You get the speed and scale of software with the judgment of a real marketing team.

How soon will I see results?

Most clients see meaningful movement in the first 30 days (new creative, CRO wins, new traffic sources), and compounding results by day 90. We set clear KPIs upfront so you can judge progress honestly.

Do you work with small businesses or just SMEs?

Both. Our engagements start from $2.5k/mo for local businesses and scale up to full fractional-CMO retainers for multi-channel SMBs.

What industries do you work with?

E-commerce, B2B SaaS, local services, agencies, and professional services are our sweet spots. We stay away from categories we can't move the needle for.

What does the free audit include?

A 30-minute call plus a written report covering your funnel, ads, SEO, analytics, and the three biggest AI opportunities specific to your business — yours to keep whether you hire us or not.

Can I just hire you for one channel?

Absolutely. Most clients start with one service (usually paid media or SEO) and expand as we prove out results.
